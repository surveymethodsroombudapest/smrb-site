To test the impact of various data collection methods, the ELTE Survey Methods Room Budapest research group commissioned three different research firms to conduct questionnaire-based data collection surveys of 1,000 respondents each between April 7 and 10, 2026. During the research, all three firms used the same questionnaire to examine election-related questions, based on their own, different methodological standards. The methods used included a variety of sampling and data collection techniques. We will present the methodological findings of the research on April 30, 2026 - below, we highlight a few interesting trends.
